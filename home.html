Building a schedule around ensuring that all your plants are watered is actually pretty difficult. Water My Plants helps to solve those problems by providing and easy to use interface for creating a plant watering schedule that will remind users to water their plants on a regular basis.

Water a Plant, Save the Planet

Create an Account

You can make your own account, to store plants, and recieve text notifications.

Get Notified!

When it's time for a plant to be watered, you will receive text notifications through Twilio sent to their phone number provided on sign up.

Its Yours!

With an account tailored for you, you can add all plants you have and love, keeping track of each one individually, editing their type, species, and their watering schedule.

Accidently Make a Forest

Whoops, with all of the extremely happy and healthy plants you so effortlessly keep up thanks to Water My Plants, you now have a forest.
